Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 1, 2025

Introduction

JSONLintPlus is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our JSON validation and formatting tool.

Information We Collect

Client-Side Processing

JSONLintPlus processes all JSON data entirely within your browser using client-side JavaScript. We do not collect, store, or transmit any JSON data you input into our tool. Your data never leaves your device.

Analytics Data

We use Google Analytics to understand how our website is used. This information helps us improve our service and may include:

  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • IP address (anonymized)

Cookies

We use minimal cookies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential cookies for site functionality
  • Analytics cookies to understand usage patterns
  • Theme preference cookies (light/dark mode)

How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain our JSON validation service
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our service
  • To ensure security and prevent abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except as described in this policy:

  • We may share anonymized, aggregated data with analytics providers
  • We may disclose information if required by law

Data Security

While we implement reasonable security measures, please note that:

  • No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure
  • We cannot guarantee absolute security of our systems

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access information we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking
  • Request a copy of your data

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@jsonlintplus.com
  • Website: Contact Form
